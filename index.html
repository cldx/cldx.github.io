Hi there,

M Name is Jörn and i'm a Pencil and Digital Artist from Germany.

I believe i got into Comics like any other Kid, one day i was looking for stuff to spend my little money on, during that time a new Superman was published in Germany, i was lost for the Art of Funnybooks ever since.

These days i create my own little Universe in my Images and Comics and i hope for you to escape and get lost in it just like i do myself.

I do lose Illustrations and Drawings whenever i feel like, so scroll on to check those below.

Who again?

Jörn Konopka is an independent Pencil and Digital Artist, father of two, lover of wife, fan of Dr.Who, addict of Coffee and digester of Bakery.
